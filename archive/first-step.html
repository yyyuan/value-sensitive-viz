Welcome to our tool!

Did you know that Artificial Intelligences (AIs) support Wikipedia? As an example, ORES (Objective Revision Evaluation Service) is an AI hosting system that Wikipedia editors use to detect vandalism, measure article quality, and organize work lists. Our tool is to help you dive deeper into it to understand how does ORES works and to achieve your goals of using ORES.

How ORES works?

ORES is a machine learning model that automatically judge the quality of edits. It takes edits as the input and process them into different features. Then it makes prediction about whether this edit is damaging or non-damaging.
